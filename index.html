Greetings to all of our graduating students, faculty, and industry partners!

Today we celebrate the achievements of our students, the importance of student-faculty relationships for project-based experiential learning, and the wonderful opportunities provided by our industry partners for real-world problem solving and creativity. STEM students graduate with a resume, not just a diploma, because of the opportunity to partner with faculty research and industry projects. When students work with faculty to solve real world problems and support our partners they unlock their potential and have real impact as they learn and stretch. I'm excited to see what our graduates do next and hope you will keep in touch!

Congratulations to our students and thank you to our faculty and sponsors!

Dr. Leslie Cornick, Dean
School of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics
